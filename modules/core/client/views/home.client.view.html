Why We Don't Promote Companies or Sell Products

EVS is an Independent 3rd Party Laboratory. Therefore we can not ethically endorse one company or its products above another. Our only concern is the purity of your oils. We wish to give everyone a starting point on their journey. That is why the only reports you will find online are EVS approved oils.

Why Are There Only Good Reports Online?

    We Believe:

  • Positive promotion is better than Negative disruption.
  • The industry benefits when companies and consumers unite.
  • We only post reports online that pass our standards.

What Can I Do To Help Fight Adulteration?

    As a Company:

  • Test every oil before you buy.
  • Use suppliers that verify their oils with a qualified, 3rd Party Laboratory.
  • Promote purity and integrity.

    As a Customer:

  • Only buy essential oils that have been tested by a Qualified and Independent 3rd party.
  • Use the EVS Database to find pure oils and companies that take testing seriously.

Which Company Should I Buy From?

As an independent third party lab we can’t answer that question. What we can do, is point you in the right direction. We want everyone to be able to find pure oils, so we have created the EVS Database to help you make informed decisions when buying.

What Does An EVS Overview Look Like?

  • Purity – Is my oil free from contaminants, diluents, synthetics, and adulterations? This is an analytical term. The best test to determine purity is Gas Chromatography Mass Spectrometry (GCMS).
  • Quality – Is my oil high quality? This is a subjective term. The best test to determine quality is organoleptically (The Smell Test).
